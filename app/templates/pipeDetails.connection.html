Connect to Salesforce via OAuth

Before copying data from Salesforce to IBM dashDB, you need to connect to Salesforce via OAuth. If you don't have admin access to Salesforce, ask your Salesforce administrator to grant it to you.

Step 1: Add your OAuth callback to Salesforce

If this is your first time setting up OAuth in Salesforce, check out our tutorial for more detailed instructions.

  1. Sign in to Salesforce
  2. Go to the OAuth? setup page, and locate the connected app page for this application
  3. Copy and paste the OAuth callback for this application into the callback field: {{oauthCallback}}

Step 2: Copy your client id and secret from Salesforce

On the same Salesforce screen, copy your client id and secret and paste them here. Then click the connect button.
