Connect to Salesforce via OAuth

Before copying data from Salesforce to IBM dashDB, you need to connect to Salesforce via OAuth. If you don't have admin access to Salesforce, ask your Salesforce administrator to grant it to you.

Add your OAuth callback to Salesforce

If this is your first time setting up OAuth in Salesforce, check out our tutorial for more detailed instructions.

  1. Sign in to Salesforce
  2. Copy and paste this OAuth callback into the callback field in your Salesforce app: {{oauthCallback}}
  3. Make sure that the callback is HTTPS

Copy your consumer key and secret from Salesforce

On the same Salesforce screen, copy your consumer key and secret and paste them here. Then click the connect button.

Make sure you have pop-up blockers disabled, and then connect.
