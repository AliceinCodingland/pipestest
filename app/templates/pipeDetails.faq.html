Are there limits the amount of data I can move?

No limits we're aware of. We tested up to 100k Salesforce records. If you go past that threshold and have problems, let us know.

What options do I have to view my data?

In addition to whatever analytics application you're using (Looker, etc), you can also view your data at rest in IBM Cloudant and IBM dashDB. Check out the tutorial for details.

Is this open source?

The Simple Data Pipe is open source! We have lots of features planned, including more connectors. Have ideas? We'd love your help. Head over to Github and get your pipe on.
