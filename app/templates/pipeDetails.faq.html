Are there limits the amount of data I can move?

We tested up to 100k Salesforce records without a problem. If you go past that threshold and have problems, let us know.

Q: What options do I have to view my data?

In addition to whatever analytics application you're using (Looker, etc), you can also view your data in Cloudant and dashDB.
